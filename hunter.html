The basics

On the hunters side, there is only you. You alone have to take on 4 survivors and prevent them from escaping.

The objective

The sole objective of the hunter is to keep all the generators dead and hunt the survivors by any means necessary.

Powers

Each hunter has their own unique power. All of them have the common theme of slowing down survivors in their own way. Here are a few examples...


Legion Trapper Huntress Wraith Plauge Add-ons
Able to hit survivors and apply deep wound status effect to slow them down. Can place bear traps around the map and catch survivors. Stopping them dead in their tracks. Throws hatchets from afar, able to wound survivors. He can cloak and be invisible to the naked eye, however he is not able to attack. He can get out of it and get a speed boost to attack. She can throw up on object, survivors, or anything interactive and can make survivors sick. There are alot of them. The add-ons can boost the powers in their own way. All of the items and add ons can be obtained from the bloodweb.
