Blindness effect

Blindness makes a survivors aura reading abilitys to not work. This can hurt the survivor in many way in many situations.

Bloodlust

Once the hunter has chased a survivor for quite some time. The hunter starts to move quicker with each level it gains. Bloodlust has 3 levels.

Broken

Once this has been applied to the survivor. They can no longer heal back to the healthy for a certain amount of time. This can make it more difficult for the survivors to survive since they are stuck injured.

Cursed

This gives survivors a heads up that there is a totem on the map that needs to be cleansed. There are many hex perks that can apply this to survivors in their own ways.

Deep Wound

Deep wound is where the survivor is injured and on their last breath. They only have a certain amount of time to mend their wounds before they are downed.

Endurance

Once this has been applied to a survivor through perks. The survivor can take one more hit before going down. Resulting in deep wound.

Exhausted

Exhausted can come from many things. From perks that make you go faster to add-ons on hunters that can apply it to you. This is here so that you dont use the same speed perk every 2 seconds. So use it wisely.

Exposed

The Exposed Status Effect puts Survivors in great peril, allowing the Killer to deal double-damage with their Basic Attack. This will put survivors in the down state instantly.

Haste

The Haste Status Effect temporarily boosts the Survivor's Movement speed.

Haemorrhage

Survivors affected by the Haemorrhage Status Effect are bleeding profusely, making it easier for Killers to track them by the Pools of Blood left on the ground.

Hindered

The Hindered Status Effect temporarily impairs the Survivor's Movement speed, the strength of which depending on the Unlockable applying the Status Effect.

Mangled

The Mangled Status Effect slows the speed at which the affected Survivor can be healed by 20 %.

Haste

Survivors affected by the Oblivious Status Effect are oblivious to the Killer's whereabouts, cloaking their Terror Radius.
